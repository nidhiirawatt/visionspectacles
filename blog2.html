Dos and Don’ts of Using Glasses Cleaners

As someone who wears glasses 24 hours a day, it is a full-time job to keep your glass lenses clean. Though we all know how difficult and significant of a task it is! We must find water, use cotton or lint-free cloth, and inspect it thoroughly while ensuring that we do not touch our fingers on the lenses. Someone should start paying for this now as it requires more dedication and time than a regular skin-care routine. So, if you wear glasses, you must also be aware of the struggle to use a glasses cleaner to keep them clean.

Do’s of Using Glasses Cleaner

Always Wash Your Hands First
Spray on Each Side of the Lenses
People often clean the outer portion of the glass and leave the insides. This will let dirt collect on your lens and the glass surface, which may lead to a severe eye infection. So, when you use glasses cleaner, spray it on both sides to keep the glass clean. You can clean one side at a time thorough cleaning.
Always Use a Clean & Soft Cloth
Rough cloth may end up scratching your glasses and looking at the world with scratchy eyeglasses is incredibly uncomfortable. Instead, use a soft cloth that has not been washed with harsh detergent or fabric softener. Do not use a dirty piece of clothing as there might be debris or dirt trapped in the cloth fiber, which will stick to your glass. Also, it is advised to use lint-free cloth, or it may stick on the glass, which is hard to get rid of!
Use a Gentle Glasses Cleaner
There are diverse types of glasses cleaners available in the market. However, their quality varies. Some glasses cleaners are gentle, while some are harsh and may ruin the quality of glasses over time. Therefore, it is advised to buy a glasses cleaner from trusted eye brands such as Titan Eye plus, which provides the best quality cleaners that help preserve the glasses for the longest time.
Buy Glasses Cleaner as Per Lens Quality
We have already mentioned that not all glass cleaners for eyeglass cleaning are the same. There are different varieties available in the market. Some are specific to the type of lenses you wear. Consult with your optometrist and buy accordingly.

Don'ts of using glass cleaners

Never Buy Local Glass Cleaners
These local brands use harsh chemicals in the glass cleaners, which will ruin your glasses. Back in the day, eyeglasses were made of glass and could withstand the test of time, whereas now the lenses are made of plastic, and if proper care is taken, they will get damaged easily. And considering people now get glasses to protect their eyes from the blue light and glare, they are quite costly too. So do not buy glasses cleaner from the local brands.
Do Not Spray Too Much
People often think spraying one more time or poring extra will make any difference. No, it does not! Spraying once is enough to clean one side. So, there is no use in wasting the entire bottle at once.
Do Not Use the Window Cleaning Spray
No, the window cleaner and eyeglass cleaning spray do not contain the same chemicals. This is because window glasses and eyeglass lenses are different and hence require a different chemical formula. So, avoid using window cleaners, too, as they can damage the lens coating.
Never Use Alcohol-containing Glasses Cleaners
Alcohol glass cleaners will damage your glass lens as well as damage your eyeglass body as well. The rubbing alcohol might make your glass shine but damage your lens. Over time, avoid using dishwashing liquids to keep your eyeglass clean.
